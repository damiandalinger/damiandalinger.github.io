Folc: The Blossom

Harness the power of enchanted fruits and uncover the truth behind a fractured world.

  • Role: Programmer & Game Designer
  • Genre: Single-player Action RPG
  • Engine: Unreal Engine 5
  • Plattform: PC
  • Duration: 5 months (Vertical Slice)

Folc: The Blossom is a story-rich, single-player action RPG set in a multi-cultural high-fantasy world torn by conflict and arcane forces. You play as Runa, a young woman from the Bone Tribe, who is forced into exile after her homeland is attacked. Struggling to survive, she journeys across hostile territories, uncovers ancient secrets, and gets caught in a dangerous political and magical web.

Players explore diverse regions, engage in fast-paced combat, and collect magical fruits to unleash powerful abilities. The world of Folc is richly atmospheric, featuring living ecosystems, NPCs and a narrative that shifts based on your actions. With every step, Runa grows stronger - facing foes, forging alliances, and ultimately leading an uprising to reclaim her family and heritage.

Ability Design & VFX

I designed and implemented four unique abilities for Folc: The Blossom, each paired with custom VFX to enhance their gameplay impact.

Thorn Zone: Launches an explosive bundle of thorns that create a lingering damage area.

Chain Pull: Summons a chain to pull enemies toward the player for follow-up strikes.

Spear Barrage: Creates and fires precise spears at targeted enemies.

Vine Hold: Summons vines to root enemies in place and deal damage over time.

Additionally, I set up the global lighting and atmospheric mood for Folc: The Blossom, designing the natural light sources like sun and ambient fog to enhance the game’s immersive tone.
