Daunted Diana

Discover dark secrets behind bright facades.

  • Role: Programmer
  • Genre: 2D Point-and-Click Adventure
  • Engine: Unity Engine
  • Plattform: PC
  • Duration: 4 months (Verticle Slice)

Set within the eerie halls of Kahneman University - a campus with a hidden past as a psychiatric clinic - the game invites players to unravel dark mysteries beneath its seemingly normal surface.

Players follow Diana, a shy but determined university student, as she partners with her friend Frank to investigate unsettling events around the campus. Through engaging dialogues, witty interactions, and environmental storytelling, players encounter a colorful cast of quirky characters while solving intricate puzzles to piece together the truth.

Blending dark humor, emotional depth, and a touch of mystery, Daunted Diana offers a character-driven narrative that explores themes of trust, fear, and personal growth.

Please note, that all screenshots and videos reflect the work of the entire team!

Inventory & Crafting System

I implemented the complete inventory and crafting system for Daunted Diana. Players can collect, combine, and use items to solve various puzzles throughout the game.

Color Mixing Brush Puzzle

I developed the unique color mixing brush puzzle featured in the game. Players rotate brushes in 90° steps, aligning their tips to mix colors like orange, purple, and green.
