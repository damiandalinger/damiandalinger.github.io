Hey, I’m Damian,

a game programmer and designer passionate about creating meaningful experiences for people - and for me, games are one of the most powerful ways to achieve that.

I’m currently studying Game Production & Management at Hochschule Neu-Ulm, where I focus on programming and game design. I’m especially drawn to the challenge of translating ideas into functional, interactive systems. Whether it’s for a university project or a personal experiment, I’m always up for something new — like building this website.

Below, you’ll find some of the projects I’ve contributed to so far. If you’re curious about anything or just want to connect, feel free to reach out!

Folc: The Blossom

Unreal Engine 5, University, Vertical Slice

A single-player, story-driven 3D action RPG set in a rich, magic-infused fantasy world. Players follow Runa on her quest to uncover her origins, navigate the tensions between warring factions, and save her mother. The game features fast-paced combat where players consume magical fruits to unleash powerful abilities.

I contributed by creating four unique abilities with custom VFX and I handled the complete lighting setup.

SmartLink

Unity, C#, University, Client Work

SmartLink is a 2D serious game developed in collaboration with the Handwerkskammer Ulm. It introduces students to electrical engineering by letting them complete realistic tasks and logic-based challenges in a smart home environment.

I built the Free Flow levels, where players solve puzzles to integrate devices into a smart system. I also developed the inventory system and the game’s first two sections.

Voivode

Unity, C#, University, Vertical Slice

Voivode is a 3D real-time tactics game set in 15th-century Wallachia, where players lead vampires and werewolves against Ottoman invaders. The game blends stealth and strategy, letting players control powerful creatures in tense tactical scenarios.

I created five enemy types using a finite state machine, enabling behaviors such as patrolling, hunting, and searching. I also implemented a JSON-based save/load system, camera controls, and the lighting.

Daunted Diana

Unity, C#, University, Vertical Slice

Daunted Diana is a 2D point-and-click adventure game that follows a shy university student as she uncovers dark secrets at Kahneman University, a campus once used as a psychiatric clinic. Players solve puzzles, meet quirky characters, and explore a story blending dark humor, mystery, and emotional depth.

I implemented the inventory and crafting system, allowing players to collect and combine items to progress. Additionally, i created a brush puzzle, where rotating and aligning brushes causes colors to change and mix.
